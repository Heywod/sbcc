Welcome to Springbrook Country Club!



Springbrook Country Club is an 18 hole private country club located in DeWitt, Iowa with a rich history that dates back to 1921. Springbrook is just a short drive from Clinton and the Quad Cities. Springbrook members enjoy a beautiful golf course, a calendar full of social activities, and casual dining in the Grill Room.



We welcome the opportunity to serve your golf and social needs. Please contact us if you would like more information or have any questions.
