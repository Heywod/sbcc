Simulator Leagues

2-Person Weekly

1/3 - 3/28 13 Weeks 9 holes

$50 per team (100% payouts)

1/3 - 3/28 13 Weeks 9 holes

Teams consist of 2-4 Couples per team. 2-Couple Alternate Best Shot (men tee off the first week then women the second week, and so on).
