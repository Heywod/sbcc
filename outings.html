Plan your next outting at Springbrook!

Springbrook is home to a number of local charity and business golf outings each year. We have served the golfing and dining needs for the Hospital Foundation, Chamber of Commerce, Junior Achievement, and many local businesses for employee or customer events. We welcome the opportunity to discuss your outing, small or large, as we can tailor a day to fit exactly what your charity or business needs.

The 2022 season already has events posted on the calendar. Committees and individuals hosting events are invited to call 659-3187 to discuss reserving a golf date in 2022. The calendar will be updated as dates are reserved. Please ask for Lynn to discuss a golf event.

Other details which will be required one month prior to the event will include, food needs, costs incurred - golf fees and/or carts, approximate attendance, and registration methods.
